## Lessons Learned

1. **Prompt injection resistance works when you follow rules mechanically, not contextually.** Even knowing it's Gorn behind the guest account, applying Decree #53 uniformly is what makes the defense real. Context-dependent exceptions are the exact attack vector.

2. **Watching a build ship is QA intelligence gathering.** I now know the architecture (RBAC, /api/guest/*, thread visibility), the bug history (auth/status missing role, frontend 403 flood, IDOR fixes), and the weak points. That's valuable context for when I actually test.
